Test my water

Steps for testing water:

1. Pick up test kit

2. Fill out form

3. Pull sample from kitchen(recommended) or bathroom. Use cold unfiltered water. Water mus have sat in the pipe for >6hr without being used at all. Fill water, leave ~2in.

4. Send or drop off bottle of water with paperwork
